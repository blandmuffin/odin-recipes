Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Description

This blueberry smoothie is a great way to start your day. It's packed with antioxidants and vitamins that will give you energy and help you start your day in the right way.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
